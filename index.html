How to Play Volleyball

Basic Rules

  • Teams of 6 players
  • 3 hits maximum per side
  • Player cannot hit the ball twice in succession
  • Ball must be returned over the net in 3 hits or less
  • Games are played to 25 points (must win by 2)

Key Skills

  • Serving (underhand or overhand)
  • Passing (forearm underhand pass)
  • Setting (overhead pass)
  • Attack options (hitting/spiking)
  • Blocking (from the front row)
  • Defensive skills (rolling, sliding)

Volleyball Fun Facts

Origin

Volleyball was invented in 1895 by William G. Morgan in Massachusetts, USA.

Olympic Sport

Volleyball became an Olympic sport in 1964 at the Tokyo Games.

Speed

A professional volleyball serve can reach speeds of up to 130 km/h (80 mph).

Beach Volleyball

Beach volleyball became an Olympic sport in 1996 at the Atlanta Games.
