Top Leagues & Players

The best volleyball competitions and athletes in the world

Major Volleyball Leagues

FIVB Volleyball Nations League

The premier annual international volleyball competition featuring the top 16 men's and women's national teams.

Italian Volleyball League (SuperLega)

Considered the strongest men's domestic volleyball league in the world, featuring many international stars.

Russian Volleyball Super League

One of the top European leagues with high-level competition and excellent facilities.

Brazilian Volleyball Superliga

The top volleyball league in South America, producing many world-class players.

Greatest Players of Recent Times

Volleyball Hall of Fame

Lang Ping (China)

The "Iron Hammer" who won Olympic gold as both player (1984) and coach (2016).

Sergey Tetyukhin (Russia)

The only male volleyball player to win medals in 5 consecutive Olympics.

Regla Torres (Cuba)

Named "Best Female Volleyball Player of the 20th Century" by FIVB.

Lorenzo Bernardi (Italy)

Considered one of the most complete players in history, leading Italy to dominance in the 1990s.
